Mission

What EcoGo is all about.

The Problem

The earth is dying. Bees are dying. Animals are becoming extinct. Oceans are polluted. We can't wait to solve all these environmental issues. The time is now!

Mission Statement

Our mission is to spread awareness on environmental issues especially those around the DMV area. We are focused on making sure that everyone is mindful and conscious of what is happening around the world and the everyday actions that they can take to protect the earth.

Why We Do This?

In recent political discussions, climate change has been a big controversial topic. However, the effects of our lack of responsibility has impacted environments immensely. We made this website to help people understand that it is not hard to help conserve the planet and to bridge the education gap on environmental issues.

Journey

Our journey and future goals.

  • June 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    This team was formed in a Girls Who Code classroom in the summer of 2017 at Georgetown University. Under the sponsorship of Lockheed Martin, we learned computer science skills that have allowed us to take this project into our own hands.

  • July 2017

    An Idea is Born

    Out of a collective love for the environment, the four of us decided to create a project that combined this passion with a brand new skill. With these skills we were able to create a website that was informational and useful for the modern adult.

  • In The Future

    Content

    Over the course of the two weeks we had to complete this project, we had to research a broad area of subjects regarding the environment. In addition to researching existing media, we were able to create our own original content. With personal projects and the help of some outside friends, we made the site a repository for new and existing media.

  • Vision

    Goals

    In the future, we hope to maintain the site and make in impact in the modern world. It would be our hope to see more original content and continued development of the site as a whole.

Spotlight

Jane Goodall

Jane Goodall is known for her study of chimpanzees in which she went in as a scientist and came out, a conservationist. After witnessing the endangerment of the chimps, Goodall decided to dedicate her life to animal activism. Goodall is considered the world’s foremost chimpanzee expert. She spent years observing a family of chimps and is credited with the first ever recorded observation of chimps eating meat and making tools (Biography). Goodall was also able to end the misconception that all chimpanzees were vegetarian when she observed their hunting and feeding skills. Jane Goodall became the author of twelve books, namely In the Shadow of Man, in addition to numerous articles. Overall, Goodall’s research has set an example of wholesome observation practices for future scientists to live by.
